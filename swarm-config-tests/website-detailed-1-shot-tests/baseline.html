Agentic Engineering

2025 marks a pivotal year for agentic AI, with Gartner naming it the top technology trend. Agentic engineering represents a paradigmatic shift in AI systems, characterized by multi-agent collaboration, dynamic task decomposition, and orchestrated autonomy.

Unlike traditional AI agents that handle narrow, task-specific automation, agentic AI systems enable multiple agents to work together, with orchestrators coordinating specialized subagents operating in parallel.
